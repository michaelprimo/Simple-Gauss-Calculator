Simple Summation Calculator

Choose a number. This App will calculate the sum of the numbers from 1 to the number you choose.

The result will appear here:

The Gauss Summation Formula allows to calculate instantaneously sums of numbers. It works like this: (n*(n+1))/2. Even if with coding it's easy to sum numbers, this application use this formula.
